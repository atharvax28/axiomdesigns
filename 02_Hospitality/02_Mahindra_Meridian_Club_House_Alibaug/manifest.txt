CLIENT   : MAHINDRA MERIDIAN – CLUB HOUSE
LOCATION : Alibaug, Raigadh
AREA     : 5,500 sq. ft.
STYLE    : Bali Style Interior
───────────────────────────────────────────
DESCRIPTION
Club house for the Mahindra Meridian complex in Alibaug. Isolated site
posed logistics challenges. Natural materials throughout: wood panels and
cane sheets for ceilings. Cement mural at the reception backdrop was the
design highlight. Insect-proofing natural materials was a key learning.
Sister project to the Mahindra Meridian Show Flat (same complex).
